# Summary
Grafana is a powerful open-source visualization tool that works seamlessly with MySQL, making database monitoring simple and effective. The included template lets teams launch production-ready dashboards quickly, providing an easy, flexible foundation for both day 1 setup and ongoing day 2 operations. 
With its open-source nature, Grafana empowers teams to customize, extend, and optimize dashboards to fit their unique needs.
